How to import products:
  1. Search for products using keywords (e.g., "iPhone", "Laptop")
  2. Browse the results from CJ Dropshipping
  3. Set a commission rate for each product
  4. Click "Import" to add the product to your marketplace
  5. The product will be automatically synced with CJ for pricing and stock
Tips for Success
  • Commission Rates: Typical rates are 5-15% depending on product category
  • Featured Products: Mark high-margin products as "Featured" for better visibility
  • Categories: Organize products into categories for easier discovery
  • Sync: Products automatically sync prices and stock from CJ
